CDC

This product is a combination of three common products that everybody has in their homes. A sofa, a table and a bed.
As you will see in the images and drawings , the cushions on the sofa can be used as stools when you use the product as a table. Under the sofa there is plenty of storage space as well as a mattress for the bed.
To use the product as a bed the user needs to open the back part of the sofa and move it to locate the whole structure in a stable position.
